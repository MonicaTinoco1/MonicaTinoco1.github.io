Human trafficking is one of largest issues occuring right now all over the world. Children, women and men are all being trafficked. The project is a collage that is life size in dimensions with a thin plywood backing. The items that are in the collage represent the things that were used to entice the person to go with the trafficker whether it was a passport to another country or drugs if that person was an addict. There are also items to represent the things they are forced to endure such as physical beatings, neglect..etc. Instead of a face, a flower was placed to represent the many persons that are/were affected by this issue. The red acrylic paint that was brushed all over the image represents the violence and death.
