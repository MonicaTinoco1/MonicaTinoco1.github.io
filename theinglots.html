The Inglot album was a fictional client commission assignment. Three separate individuals were asked to pose for this fictional rock band. I chose to use animal masks to create the bandâ€™s individual aesthetic. The clothing worn on the models was their own. For typography, I chose a style that would add a grunge band's name and the overall album cover style as well. The back cover I created two options that were simplistic and complementary to the front cover. One of the back covers has just a centered list of songs with the copyright trademark information on the bottom. The other back cover has the list of songs wrapped around the centered band members masks.
